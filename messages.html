Pastor E.A. Adeboye                         Topic: Carriers of His Glory                     Text: Isaiah 60:1-9.

Introduction
- The Glory of God is as light - Luke 2:9.
- You carrying His glory means you're shining your light - Matthew 5:14-16.

- As light, when you appear darkness must disappear - 2 Kings 2:19-22.
- As light, sickness disappears when you appear - Acts 9:32-35, 9:36-42.
- As light, Barrenness must disappear - 2 Kings 4:8-17.
- It is important to know that if you are a carrier of God's glory, the Glory is for God and not you. (He will never share His glory with anyone).


- As a carrier of God's glory, you will benefit - Matthew 21:1-9.
- Conclusively; once you start carrying His glory, do not stop.

Prayer Point:
- Father, let me be a carrier of your glory for the rest of my life.

 Pastor E. A. Adeboye                     Text: Psalm 30:5-6             Topic: Joy Cometh in the morning


Everyday starts in the evening - Genesis 1:5
Between every two days there's a night - Genesis 1:8

- If you are passing through a night season look forward the day will break.
Take courage the night would soon be over.

- God has the ability to make your day time a long time - Psalm 90:4.
Your day could last till you get to heaven - Revelation 21:25.

- The Children of Israel had been in darkness for four hundred and thirty years - Exodus 12:30-36.

- Another night at the red sea Exodus 14:5-31

1 Samuel 30:1-36.

- Whenever you find yourself in a tough situation, cry unto the Lord - John 10:10, Mark 5:35-43.

Prayer
Father, prince of peace, every storm in my family let it end today.
